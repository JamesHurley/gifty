What's the story?

Managing gift wishlists, especially around the holidays, has always been a nightmarish task for members of my large family. Constant back-and-forth emails, phone calls, and the occasional verbal assualt were all ways we dealt with this madness. There had to be a better way...

And so, Gifty was born.

Now, sharing and reserving gifts for your friends and family is easy, fast, and painless. Check it out for free today!
